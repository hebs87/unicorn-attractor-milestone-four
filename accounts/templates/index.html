About UnicornAttractor

A magical Unicorn collecting game – find these mythical creatures and enjoy their company each time you open the game! Love Unicorns? Then this is the game for you!

How to play UnicornAttractor

Place food and magic dust and wait for Unicorns to come and play!

Create your own magical Unicorn companion

Pick a dedicated Unicorn to be your lifelong companion. You can treat them to a wide range of clothing and accessories and create the most exclusive Unicorn with unique styles. You can also play mini games with your furry friend every day!

Create your fantasy Unicorn world

Place food and magic dust to make your Unicorn world full of wonder and amazement. Use them to attract other Unicorns to your world so your companion has lots of friends.

Complete challenges

When you’re not busy playing with your pals, complete mini puzzle challenges to earn more magic dust. You can then use your magic dust to buy clothes and accessories for your companion, or to attract new friends to your world!
